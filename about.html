About Our Company

THE COMPANY

Unique General Contracting and Trading is a dedicated service oriented company based out of Saudi Arabia, which provides multiple industrial services and material supply with unbeatable quality.
We are a group of innovative and enthusiastic engineers, pioneering the mechanical industry with more than a decade’s experience in providing technical assistance, support and maintenance. With our team’s first-hand knowledge in instrumentation, mechanical and industrial engineering, project management and material science, we can be counted as one of the most reliable and robust companies in GCC. Headquartered in Saudi Arabia we have branches in Bahrain & UAE.

VISION

As a company highly committed towards customer satisfaction and professionalism, our vision is to be the leader in being a reliable one stop for customers looking for a guaranteed and devoted assistance in project undertaking, professional manpower supply, industrial material supply, providing technical support and construction services by setting an international benchmark in the industry.
